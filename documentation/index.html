	If you wish to buy your own drone and fly it in your community, there are some general guidelines you have to adhere to before you can do it. If your drone weighs over 0.55 pounds, you will need to register it with the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). After that, you can go ahead and fly your drone. There are some general rules you need to keep in mind.
	Always fly below 400 feet and make sure you can see your drone at all time. Stay away from low- flying aircrafts, like helicopters, and keep away from tracks and stadiums. Make sure you drone stay away from other hazardous objects in the sky or close to the ground.
	Drones can be used for recreational uses and business uses. For recreational uses, you can use your drone as a hobby. Purposes include putting a camera on the drone and filming stuff. If you are videoing to advertise, then that is for commercial use and is not considered for recreational use. For business uses, you must have expressed permission from the FAA before you can fly your drone. All of these information can be found at knowbeforeyoufly.org. You must know your limits and rules in order to fly your drone.
